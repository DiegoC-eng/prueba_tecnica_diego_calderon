Risk 1: Stock-out Crisis

📦 309,771 gaps of 3+ days without sales detected

💸 $339M in estimated lost GMV over the period

📈 Average gap duration: 5.2 days

🏪 Most affected: Electronics ($112M) and Home ($99M)

Impact: This is the single biggest revenue risk. Even recovering 20% of lost GMV = +$67M.

Risk 2: Loyalty Retention Drop

👥 54% of loyal customers don't return in Month 1

📉 Month 6 retention: only 21-26%

💰 Each lost loyal customer = avg $105/month in GMV

📊 The A/B test showed the new exhibition did not significantly increase GMV (p=0.241)

Impact: Without re-engagement, we lose 74% of loyal customers within 6 months.

Risk 3: Data Quality Gaps

🔍 59.8% anonymous transactions (no customer_id)

⚠️ 1.0% total_amount discrepancies vs item sums

❌ 2 stores in both A/B groups (TIENDA_008, TIENDA_037) — compromised experiment

📅 A/B groups were not comparable pre-test (p=0.050)

Impact: A/B test results are inconclusive. Cannot recommend rollout of new exhibition with current data.

A/B Test Conclusion: Do NOT roll out the new exhibition yet.

Result: p=0.241 (not significant). Lift: -17% GMV in TREATMENT vs CONTROL. But groups were not balanced pre-test (CONTROL stores had $43K more GMV/store in pre-period). Recommendation: Redesign experiment with balanced randomization and run for at least 8 weeks.

👤 Owner: CRM & Marketing Manager 📅 Deadline: July 31, 2025 💰 Potential upside: +$567K/month in retained GMV
3️⃣

Operational Turnaround Plan for 10 BAJO_RENDIMIENTO Stores

10 stores are below P25 GMV/m² in their format. Root cause analysis per store: assortment gaps, pricing, staffing, or location. If these stores reach P50, total uplift = +$2.1M per quarter.

👤 Owner: Regional Operations VP 📅 Deadline: September 30, 2025 💰 Potential upside: +$8.4M/year
4️⃣

Redesign A/B Test with Proper Controls — Do Not Roll Out Exhibition Yet

Current test is not valid: groups are unbalanced (-$43K/store pre-test difference) and 2 stores are in both groups. Redesign with stratified randomization by format and country. Run for minimum 8 weeks (not 6). Required statistical power: 80% with α=0.05.

👤 Owner: Merchandising + Data Team 📅 Deadline: New test launch: October 1, 2025 💰 Risk avoided: Incorrect rollout cost to 40 stores
